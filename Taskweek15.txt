Task 1 :

Your task is to spawn multiple objects, each with different points.

For instance:

A Cone has 5 points
A Cube has 3 points
A Cylinder has 2 points
Then, interact with these objects, pick them up, and print out the accumulated points and the number of pickups either as a log, on-screen debug or create a UI if you have time.


Task 2: 

Create a road, you can start exploring epic Gamestore for road and vehicle models. 
drive your vehicle ( or cube) and try to avoid drops that are falling from the sky on the road in a random manner.
Your car has a HP of 100, every time you hit a drop minus 1 point from the HP.
